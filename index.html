Privacy Policy

Your photos stay on
your device. Always.

Culla is designed from the ground up to keep your memories private. There is no server, no account, no analytics, and no way for any data to leave your device — because no such code exists in the app.

This policy applies to Culla for iOS, developed by Alejandro Gómez Urrea.

What we collect

  • Nothing. Culla does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

Photo library access

  • Culla requests access to your photo library solely to display and organize photos on-device.
  • Photos are referenced by asset identifier only — they are never copied, moved, or uploaded.
  • You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings.

Data storage

  • Sorting metadata (gallery names, asset identifiers) is stored locally using SwiftData.
  • This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Third parties

  • Culla contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking libraries of any kind.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. As long as Culla remains local-only, this policy will remain the same.
